About Me Section

Welcome!!!! I'm Shani :), I'm a passionate artist who finds inspiration in the timeless works of Monet, Odilon Redon, and Takashi Murikami. I love poetry and I'm proud of the rich tapestry of my יהודי (being Jewish) and Black heritage. I'm from Bronzeville, and in my free time, I love to cook and draw.

School

Why I am interested in art

Ever since I was little it seemed like I had a lifelong dedication to art. I appreciate how Art opens the mind to possibilities, supports the imagination, and allows you to experience the world differently. I've formulated this definition through my encounters with amazing and influential artists such as Nick Cave and early collaborations with Theaster Gates, this has not only honored my artistic skill but also nurtured a profound connection to the world around me. Through visual expression, I've been able to unlock various possibilities, perceptions, and historical contexts that shape both personal awareness and societal understanding. My art isn't just about strokes on a canvas. It is a portrait of different experiences and imagination. My art isn't just personal.
